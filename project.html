Meet the characters!


Tom Nook
In New Horizons, Nook acts as the head of Nook Incorporated. He acts as the leader on the island, acting as task giver and island upgrader.


Timmy and Tommy Nook
Timmy buys and sells items inside the Resident Services tent, while Tommy wanders outside with a flag and provides tips. It is notable that Tommy has a slightly higher pitched voice than Timmy, since he is younger than his twin Timmy.
